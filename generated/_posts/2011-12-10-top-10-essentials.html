I recently read about the top 10 essentials of hiking. Could be useful to know for the future.

  1. Map
  2. Compass
  3. Sunglasses and sunscreen
  4. Extra food and water
  5. Extra clothes
  6. Headlamp / flashlight
  7. First aid kit
  8. Fire starter
  9. Matches
  10. Knife
